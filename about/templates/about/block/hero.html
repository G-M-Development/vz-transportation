About us

  • V Z TRANSPORTATION Trucking Inc was established in 2013 when 23 years old Vadym Zelenko and Alexandra Zelenko found that trucking is his passion.

  • A young emigrant tried himself in the restaurant and construction industry, which wasn’t his thing, and then he finally got his CDL, and from day 1 he fell in love with trucking, especially in an open deck, heavy haul industry.

  • 1st driver was hired in 2014 by Vadym, and that’s how the story began. After 10 years company got more than *60 trucks and *70 trailers. In 2021 V Z TRANSPORTATION Trucking Inc. (Vadym Zelenko and Alexandra Zelenko) decided to move to a new, beautiful building in Kyiv.

  • Thanks to G&M developer, we can ensure a higher level of reliability and safety for our transportation, allowing us to meet the needs of our customers and drivers while reducing the number of accidents and delays in our operations. Every day we analyze our work, improving and doing our 100% to be the best. We built a great team and excellent reputation, and we truly believe that V Z TRANSPORTATION.
